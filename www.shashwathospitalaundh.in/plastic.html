Plastic Surgery

Plastic surgery encompasses a variety of procedures aimed at restoring function or enhancing appearance. Here are some common procedures:

Cleft Lip

Cleft lip surgery repairs a congenital split in the upper lip, restoring its normal appearance and function.

Cleft Palate

Surgery for a cleft palate corrects the gap in the roof of the mouth, improving speech, eating, and overall quality of life.

Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation enhances the size and shape of the breasts using implants, often performed for cosmetic reasons or reconstructive purposes.

Liposuction

Liposuction removes excess fat from specific areas of the body, improving body contours and proportions.

Z-Plasty for Pilonidal Sinus

Z-plasty is a technique used to treat pilonidal sinus by repositioning skin flaps to close the sinus tract, promoting healing and reducing recurrence.
