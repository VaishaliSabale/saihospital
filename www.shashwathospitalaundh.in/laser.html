Laser Surgery

Laser surgery is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat various conditions with precision and reduced recovery time. It is particularly effective for treating anorectal disorders.

Piles (Hemorrhoids)

Laser surgery for piles involves shrinking hemorrhoids using a focused laser beam, resulting in minimal pain and faster recovery.

Fissure

Laser treatment for anal fissures helps in healing the tear in the lining of the anus with reduced discomfort and a quicker return to normal activities.

Fistula

Laser surgery for fistulas effectively closes the abnormal connection between the anus and the skin, promoting faster healing with minimal complications.

Pilonidal Sinus

Laser treatment for pilonidal sinus involves removing the sinus tract with precision, reducing the risk of recurrence and speeding up the healing process.
